User research

Target audience

My target audience includes individuals involved in the production, marketing, and promotion of cultural and creative products, as well as those seeking to collaborate with IPs in the cultural and creative sector.

This report aims to provide them with up-to-date data and insights to support more informed decision-making, regarding which products to prioritize, what to produce more or less of, how to approach pricing strategies, and how to identify suitable IPs for collaborations with traditional cultural sites or museums.

Interview script

Although I may not be able to directly interview individuals currently working in this specific field, I plan to consult people with experience in areas such as art design and marketing. I will ask for their feedback on whether this report makes sense, whether the data is useful, and if it can support decision-making in real-world contexts.

Their insights will help evaluate the practical value of this research and indicate whether the findings could be applied to product development, marketing strategies, or potential collaborations with cultural institutions.
